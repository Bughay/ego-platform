The App

Everything your coach fills in for you

The coach does the work, but the full app is yours to control. Track, build and review every part of your training and nutrition.

🍎

Nutrition

Build your own food database with macros stored per 100g. Log a meal by saying what you ate and how many grams, and the calories, protein, carbs and fat totals are computed for you automatically.

🏋

Training

Create reusable workout routines, then log workouts as they happen. Every exercise records its working weight in kilograms and reps, and you can review any single day or a full date range.

🥘

Recipes

Compose recipes from the foods already in your database, with a gram weight per ingredient. The nutrition of a full dish is always derived from its parts, so your numbers stay honest.

📊

Analytics

Pick a date range and see a clean summary of your training and diet: macro totals, daily averages, days logged, workout count, total reps and training volume.

Manual Mode

Log, create and analyze it yourself

Prefer to drive? You are always in full control. Log, create and analyze every part of your training, nutrition and recipes by hand. The coach is there when you want it and out of your way when you do not.

📝

Log

Record your meals, workouts and daily intake as you go. Enter a food and how many grams and your calories and macros are totaled for you, the same for every workout you finish.

🛠

Create

Build your own food database, compose recipes from those foods by gram weight, and design reusable training routines, all by hand and saved to your account.

📈

Analyze

Pick any date range and review your training and nutrition together: macro totals, daily averages, days logged, workout count, total reps and training volume.
